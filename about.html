Singhs Print is a Montreal-based custom apparel studio specializing in DTG, DTF, and embroidery. We help brands, businesses, creators, and teams bring their ideas to life with honest pricing, fast turnaround, and quality you can see and feel.

Who we are

A studio that actually cares about your order

We started Singhs Print because we were tired of seeing custom apparel done poorly: slow turnarounds, inconsistent quality, and shops that treat small orders like an inconvenience.

Every order that comes through our door gets the same attention, whether it's 5 tees for a birthday or 500 hoodies for a brand launch. We print everything in-house in Montreal, which means we control the quality from start to finish.

What we do

DTG, DTF & Embroidery under one roof

Having all three printing methods in-house means we can recommend the right approach for your project instead of forcing everything through one method. Cotton tees? DTG. Polyester jerseys? DTF. Corporate polos with your logo? Embroidery.

We also have a design studio on-site for cleanups, mockups, and brand assets, so even if your artwork isn't print-ready, we'll get it there.

Who we work with

From startups to schools to Saturday markets

Our clients include local businesses needing staff uniforms, content creators launching merch, gyms and cafes with branded gear, schools and event organizers, and artists selling at pop-ups.

Whether you need 1 item or 1,000+, there's no minimum and no judgment. We've built our process to be friendly for first-timers and efficient for repeat clients.
