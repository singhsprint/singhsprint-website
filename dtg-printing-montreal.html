DTG: photo detail, no minimum

What DTG is best at, where it beats DTF and screen printing, and when we'd recommend another method.

What DTG is

Direct-to-garment prints your design straight into the fabric like an inkjet printer for fabric. The ink soaks in for a soft, no-feel finish with full photographic detail and unlimited colours - no setup screens, so one-off prints are easy and affordable.

When DTG wins

DTG is ideal for full-colour, detailed or photographic art on light cotton tees, and for one-offs and small runs where screen-print setup wouldn't make sense. It's the softest print we offer - you barely feel it on the shirt.

When we'd suggest otherwise

For dark or synthetic garments, DTF gives more vivid, durable results. For large solid-colour runs of 250+ identical tees, screen printing is cheaper per unit. We'll point you to the better fit when it applies.

FAQ

DTG printing questions we get

What is DTG printing?

DTG (direct-to-garment) prints your design directly into the fabric using specialized water-based inks, like an inkjet printer for clothing. It produces soft, detailed, full-colour prints with no setup screens, which makes it perfect for one-off and small-batch tees.

Can you print just one t-shirt?

Yes. DTG has no minimum and no setup fees, so a single custom tee is easy and affordable - priced at the 1-9 tier. It's our go-to method for one-offs and gifts.

Is DTG or DTF better for my order?

DTG is best for full-colour detailed art on light cotton and for the softest possible feel. DTF is better for dark garments, polyester and blends, and mixed orders. Send your art and garments and we'll recommend the right method.

What garments work best with DTG?

100% cotton and high-cotton blends in lighter colours give the best DTG results. For dark or synthetic garments we typically recommend DTF for more vivid, durable colour.

How do I care for a DTG-printed shirt?

Turn it inside out, wash cold, and avoid harsh dryers. Cared for that way, DTG prints stay soft and vivid through regular wear and washing.
