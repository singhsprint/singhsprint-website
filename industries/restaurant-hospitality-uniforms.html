FAQ

Questions restaurant operators ask us

How fast can you turn around new-hire reorders?

5 business days from the moment you email us the name, size, and quantity. Your embroidery file, color spec, and previous order details stay on file — no need to re-quote.

Do you supply the aprons and polos, or do we?

Either works. Default is we source: Spun, Mercer Culinary, Port Authority, or trade-channel hospitality brands. Bring-your-own-garment (BYOG) gets a $3/unit decoration discount if you already work with a supplier.

What’s the minimum order?

No hard minimum, but our tier pricing kicks in at 5 units. Most restaurants land at 15–40 units depending on team size. We’ll quote any size, but 25+ unlocks the best per-unit rate.

Embroidery vs. printed logo — which lasts longer?

Embroidery, every time, for restaurant apparel. Printed logos eventually crack or peel after enough commercial-wash cycles. Embroidery survives 50+ washes with no visible degradation. For aprons and polos we recommend embroidery by default.

Can we order one of each size as a fitting kit?

Yes — we send a fitting kit (one of every standard size) on the first order. Team tries them on, marks their size, returns the kit. From then on, your team’s sizing data lives in your account.

Do you offer Net 30?

Standard terms for new accounts: 50% deposit to start production, balance Net 30 from invoice date. For repeat accounts (after 2–3 successful orders) we offer full Net 30 with no deposit.
