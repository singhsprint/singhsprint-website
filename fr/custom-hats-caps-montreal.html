What you get

Caps and toques that wear in, not out

Why embroidery is the standard for headwear, the styles we stock, and when a printed patch makes more sense.

Embroidery is the headwear standard

Caps and toques get worn hard and washed often. Embroidered logos hold their shape and colour for years - the raised, stitched finish reads as quality and won't crack or peel the way a printed hat can.

Styles for every order

Structured and unstructured caps, snapbacks, trucker mesh, dad hats, knit beanies and toques, and visors. We source from trade-channel headwear brands, or decorate caps you supply at the same per-piece rate.

When a patch fits better

For thick layered logos or a vintage look, an embroidered or woven patch heat-applied to the cap can be the move. For very large or photographic cap art, we'll talk through printed or patch options instead of stitching.

FAQ

Custom hat questions we get

Do you embroider or print hats?

We embroider - it's the durable, premium standard for headwear. Embroidered logos keep their shape and colour through years of wear and washing. For thick layered or vintage looks we also offer embroidered and woven patches applied to caps.

Is there a minimum order for custom caps?

No minimum. We'll embroider a small batch or a few hundred. Per-unit price drops with quantity, with the best rate at 200+. Your saved stitch file makes reorders fast.

What hat styles can you customize?

Structured and unstructured caps, snapbacks, trucker mesh, dad hats, knit beanies, toques and visors. We source from trade headwear brands, or decorate caps you supply at the same per-piece rate.

Can you embroider caps I already have?

Yes. Bring your own caps and we'll embroider them at the same per-piece rate, after a quick check that the cap can be hooped or run on our cap frames.

How much does a custom embroidered cap cost?

From $32.95 each at 5-9 units down to $15.95 each at 200+, blank and front-centre logo included. Multi-location and specialty caps are quoted to match.
