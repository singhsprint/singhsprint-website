DTF: the most versatile print method

What DTF is, when it beats DTG and screen printing, and the few cases where we'd point you elsewhere.

What DTF is

Direct-to-film prints your design onto a film, then heat-presses it onto the garment. The result is a vivid, slightly raised print with a soft stretch - it bends and recovers without cracking, and the colours pop even on black garments.

When DTF wins

DTF is our go-to for dark garments, polyester and performance fabrics, nylon, blends, and anything DTG can't print well. It's also ideal for small and mixed orders - different garments and sizes in one run, no per-garment setup.

When we'd suggest otherwise

For very large solid-colour runs (250+ identical tees), screen printing can be cheaper per unit. For an ultra-soft, no-feel print on white cotton, DTG can edge it out. We'll tell you when another method serves you better.

FAQ

DTF printing questions we get

What is DTF printing?

DTF (direct-to-film) prints your design onto a special film, which is then heat-pressed onto the garment. It produces vivid, durable, slightly stretchy prints that work on virtually any fabric and any garment colour - including dark and synthetic materials.

Is DTF better than DTG?

Neither is universally better - they suit different jobs. DTF prints on any fabric and colour and is great for small, mixed and synthetic orders. DTG gives an ultra-soft, no-feel print but works best on light cotton. We recommend the right one for your garments and art.

How durable are DTF prints?

DTF holds up well through regular washing when cared for properly - turn garments inside out and wash cold. The print stretches and recovers without cracking, which is why it's popular for performance and athletic wear.

Can you DTF print on dark or polyester garments?

Yes - that's where DTF shines. It prints opaque, vivid colour on black, navy and any dark garment, and bonds well to polyester, nylon and blends where other methods struggle.

Do you sell DTF transfers without the garment?

Yes. We can produce transfer-only (gang) sheets for you to press yourself, quoted separately from decorated-garment orders. Tell us your sizes and quantities for a price.
