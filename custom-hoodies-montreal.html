What you get

Hoodies that hold up wash after wash

Print or embroidery, the blanks we recommend, and why the decoration method matters most on heavyweight fleece.

Print or embroidery

Big front graphics and gradients go DTG or DTF. Logos, crests and left-chest brand marks go embroidered for a premium, tactile finish that survives years of washing. We'll tell you which suits your design and budget.

Blanks built to last

Hoodies live a hard life, so the blank matters. We source heavyweight fleece from Gildan, Independent Trading, Comfort Colors and Canadian-made options - then match the decoration to the fabric so nothing cracks or peels.

Mockup-first on fleece

Thick fleece and seams can shift placement. You approve a photoreal mockup so you can see exactly how the print or stitch sits before we run the order. Want a printed sample first? Add one at checkout for the cost of a blank.

FAQ

Custom hoodie questions we get

Can you embroider hoodies as well as print them?

Yes. We do both in-house. Embroidery is ideal for logos and left-chest brand marks - it's premium and extremely durable. Printing (DTG or DTF) is better for large front graphics, photo art and gradients. We'll recommend based on your design.

Is there a minimum order for custom hoodies?

No minimum. We'll print a single hoodie or a few hundred. Per-unit price drops with quantity, with the best rate at 200+.

How much does a custom hoodie cost in Montreal?

A single-side printed hoodie ranges from $37.95 each at 5-9 units down to $24.95 each at 200+, blank included. Premium blanks and embroidery are quoted to match. Your exact price comes with the quote.

What hoodie blanks do you offer?

Heavyweight and mid-weight fleece from Gildan, Independent Trading, Comfort Colors, Champion and Canadian-made Rue Saint-Patrick, among others. Bring your own blanks and we'll decorate them at the same per-piece rate.

How long does a custom hoodie order take?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days from approved artwork. Tell us your deadline and we'll confirm rush options at the quote stage.
