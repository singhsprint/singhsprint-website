Accessibility Statement

Effective date: June 5, 2026

Imprimerie Singhs Print is committed to making singhsprint.com usable by as many people as possible, including people who rely on assistive technologies. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA.

1. What we do

  • Use semantic HTML and descriptive labels so screen readers can navigate the site;
  • Provide text alternatives for meaningful images;
  • Support keyboard navigation for interactive elements;
  • Aim for sufficient colour contrast and readable text sizes;
  • Offer the site in both English and French.

2. Ongoing work

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. Some third-party content (such as embedded maps or social widgets) may not fully meet our standards, and we are continually reviewing and improving the site. If you find a barrier, please tell us — your feedback helps us prioritize fixes.

3. Tell us about a barrier

If you have trouble using any part of this site, or need information in an alternative format, contact us and we will help and work to fix the issue:

Email: sales@singhsprint.com
Phone: 438-544-3800

We try to respond to accessibility requests within 5 business days.

4. Need help placing an order?

If completing an order online is difficult for any reason, call or email us and we will be glad to take your order and answer questions directly.
