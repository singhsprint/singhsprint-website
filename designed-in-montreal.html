About Rue Saint-Patrick

Founded by Ivan Green — a County Down native with more than 40 years in the garment trade — and built alongside his sons, Rue Saint-Patrick launched in 2017 as North America's first online Irish menswear brand.

It takes its name from the street that runs along Montreal's Lachine Canal through Pointe-Saint-Charles, the heart of one of North America's oldest Irish communities. The label pairs traditional tailoring with modern, easy-care fabrics — quality menswear, designed in Montreal.

We carry the smooth, decoratable pieces from their line — tees, henleys, polos, hoodies, fleece, sweaters and caps — and decorate them in-house for your team, brand or event.
