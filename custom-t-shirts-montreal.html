FAQ

Custom t-shirt questions we get

Is there a minimum order for custom t-shirts?

No. We print a single shirt or a thousand. Small orders are priced per the 5-9 tier; per-unit price drops as quantity rises, with the best rate at 250+.

Which printing method is best for my t-shirts?

It depends on your art and quantity. DTG (direct-to-garment) is best for full-colour, detailed designs on cotton and small runs. DTF transfers work on any fabric including dark and synthetic tees. Screen printing is most cost-effective for high-volume solid-colour runs of 50+. We recommend the right one when you send your art.

How fast can I get custom t-shirts in Montreal?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days from approved artwork. Rush options are available for West Island and Montreal pickups - tell us your deadline and we'll confirm at the quote stage.

Can you supply the t-shirts, or do I bring my own?

Both. We source blanks from S&S Activewear, SanMar, AlphaBroder and Canadian-made Rue Saint-Patrick - Gildan, Bella+Canvas, Comfort Colors, Next Level and more. Or bring your own garments and we'll decorate them at the same per-piece rate.

What file types do you accept for artwork?

PNG, JPG, PDF, AI, PSD and SVG. A transparent PNG or vector file is ideal. If your art isn't print-ready, we include free design help to get it there.

Do you offer a first-order discount?

Yes - your first order of $100 or more is $20 off, applied automatically when you request a quote. No code needed.
