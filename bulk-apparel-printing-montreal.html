FAQ

Bulk printing questions we get

How much does bulk t-shirt printing cost in Montreal?

A single-side printed tee runs $14.95 each at 25-49 units, $11.95 at 100-249, and $9.95 at 250+, blank and setup included. Hoodies, polos and caps follow their own volume curves. Your exact tier price comes with the quote.

What's the best method for a large order?

For high-volume solid-colour runs, screen printing gives the lowest per-unit price. For mixed garments, dark or synthetic fabrics, DTF is more practical. For logos on uniforms, embroidery is best. We match the method to your run so you get the best value.

Do you offer business terms for bulk orders?

Yes. New business accounts run on a 50% deposit to start production with the balance Net 30. After a few successful orders we can extend full Net 30. We also provide account management and keep your specs on file.

Do you deliver bulk orders?

Free delivery across Montreal on orders of 25+ units, and Canada-wide shipping for larger runs. Local pickup in Sainte-Anne-de-Bellevue is always available.

Can you handle reorders and new-hire top-ups?

Yes. Your design files, decoration specs and sizing stay on file, so reorders ship fast at the same per-unit price as your original tier - ideal for staff uniforms and ongoing merch programs.

Is there a maximum order size?

No practical maximum - we produce runs from a few dozen to several thousand. For very large or deadline-driven orders, send an RFP and we'll build a production schedule around your date.
