Cookie Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

This policy explains how Imprimerie Singhs Print uses cookies and similar technologies on singhsprint.com, and how you can control them. For more on how we handle personal information, see our Privacy Policy.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on your device by your browser. We also use similar technologies such as pixels and local storage. They help the site work, remember your choices, and — only with your consent — measure traffic and advertising.

3. Third parties

Analytics and advertising cookies are set by Google (Analytics, Ads, Tag Manager) and Meta. These providers may process data outside Quebec. We load them only after consent, using Google Consent Mode so that no analytics or advertising storage is used until you accept.
